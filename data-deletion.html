Data Deletion Request

MyMedVault respects your right to control your personal data. You can delete your data directly within the app, or submit a request by email.

Option 1 — Delete data inside the MyMedVault app

1
Delete individual documents Open any document → tap the delete icon. The document and all extracted health records associated with it are permanently removed immediately.
2
Delete your entire account Go to Home → account icon (top-right) → Delete Account. This permanently deletes your account, all uploaded documents, all extracted health records, and your email address from our servers.

Both actions take effect immediately and cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Submit a deletion request by email

If you no longer have access to the app, email us at the address below. Include the email address associated with your MyMedVault account and we will delete your data within 7 business days.

Send Deletion Request Email

What data is deleted vs. retained

Data typeWhen deletedRetention after deletion
Uploaded medical documents (files) Immediately on request None — permanently removed
Extracted health records & AI summaries Immediately on request None — permanently removed
Account (email address) Immediately on account deletion None — permanently removed
Vaccination reminder preferences When app is uninstalled or account deleted Stored locally on device only — never on our servers
Server access logs Not individually deletable Auto-deleted after 30 days (Cloud Run default)
